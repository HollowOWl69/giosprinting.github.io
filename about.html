About Us

Gio's Printing Services was established in 2003 by Mr. Jessie "Dodoy" de Jesus. From the beginning, the company's primary products were printing services and xerox. The business quickly gained a reputation for quality work and excellent customer service, which helped it establish a loyal customer base. As the business grew, the company moved to a larger location to better accommodate its customers' needs. This was a major turning point for Gio's Printing Services, as it allowed the company to expand its services and take on more clients. The move also helped to improve the company's visibility in the local community. In 2018, Gio's Printing Services was passed down to Mr. Christian Jay B. de Jesus, who has since led the company with a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction. The business continues to evolve by adopting new technologies and expanding its services, maintaining its reputation as a trusted provider of high-quality printing services and a leader in the local business community.


MISSION
to provide high-quality printing services to our customers with exceptional customer service. We aim to be a reliable and trustworthy partner for our customers in meeting their printing needs, whether it's for personal or business purposes. Our goal is to exceed our customers' expectations by delivering exceptional printing products and services that are affordable, timely, and environmentally friendly.


VISION
to become the go-to printing shop for anyone seeking top-quality printing services. We aim to be recognized for our reliability, affordability, and commitment to excellence. We aspire to continuously improve our products and services through innovative technologies and a passionate team of experts. Ultimately, we envision a world where printing is not just a service, but an experience that inspires creativity and brings ideas to life.
